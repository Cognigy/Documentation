When there is a significant increasing or decreasing trend it will be displayed next to the indcator value in a percentage figure related to the average value of the selected time period.
Increasing trends will be indicated with plus and a green colored figure, decreasing trends will be indicated with minus in red color.

## Report Indicators

### Conversations

In the **Reports** window click indicator **Conversations**.

At the bottom of the screen a bar chart diagram will show the total number of conversations that have occurred during the selected time period. When hovering the mouse cursor over a bar chart, the details of conversations that happened can be seen.
You can display the values per day, week, month or year depending on the selected time period. See [Overview](overview.md).

### Incoming Messages

In the **Reports** window click indicator **Incoming Messages**.

At the bottom of the screen a bar chart diagram will show the total number of incoming messages that have occurred during the selected time period. When hovering the mouse cursor over a bar chart, the details of incoming messages that happened can be seen.
You can display the values per day, week, month or year depending on the selected time period. See [Overview](overview.md).

### Outgoing Messages

In the **Reports** window click indicator **Outgoing Messages**.

At the bottom of the screen a bar chart diagram will show the total number of outgoing messages that have occurred during the selected time period. When hovering the mouse cursor over a bar chart, the details of outgoing messages that happened can be seen.
You can display the values per day, week, month or year depending on the selected time period. See [Overview](overview.md).

### First Response Time

In the **Reports** window click indicator **First Response Time**.

At the bottom of the screen a bar chart diagram will show the average number of time units taken, e.g. in minutes and seconds, to give the first response to a conversation with a customer. In addition, when hovering the mouse cursor over a bar chart, the average number of time units, that have taken for the first response for that specific day, can be seen.
You can display the values per day, week, month or year depending on the selected time period. See [Overview](overview.md).

### Resolution Time

In the **Reports** window click indicator **Resolution Time**.

At the bottom of the screen a bar chart diagram will show the average number of time units taken, e.g. in days and hours, to resolve conversations with a customer. In addition, when hovering the mouse cursor over a bar chart, the average number of time units, that have taken for resolving a conversation for that specific day, can be seen.
You can display the values per day, week, month or year depending on the selected time period. See [Overview](overview.md).

Click on the Resolution Time tab in Reports. A graph will show the average hours to resolve a particular Conversation with a customer. When hovering the mouse cursor over a specific chart, the average number of hours taken for determining conversations for that particular day can be seen.

### Resolution Count

In the **Reports** window click indicator **Resolution Count**.

At the bottom of the screen a bar chart diagram will show the total number of conversations that were resolved on the selected time period. When hovering the mouse cursor over a particular chart, the number of conversations, resolved on that day, can be seen.

## Bar Chart diagram

This diagramm visualizes the metrics, based on the indicator values on top. Click on an indicator value and you will get displayed the corresponding data in a bar chart diagram.

## Download Conversations Report

You can download the details of Conversations' metrics by clicking **Download agent reports** at the top right of the page. 
The report will be downloaded in CSV format.
